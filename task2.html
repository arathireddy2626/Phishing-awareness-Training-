Phishing Awareness Training

Introduction

Phishing is a type of cyber attack where attackers attempt to deceive individuals into providing sensitive information...

Types of Phishing Attacks

1. Email Phishing: Attackers use fake emails to trick recipients...

2. Spear Phishing: Targeted phishing attacks aimed at specific individuals...

3. Whaling: Attacks targeting high-profile individuals...

4. Smishing: Phishing attacks via SMS/text messages...

5. Vishing: Phishing attacks conducted over the phone...

Recognizing Phishing Emails

Common signs of phishing emails include unusual sender addresses, generic greetings, urgent or threatening language, and suspicious links or attachments...

Avoiding Phishing Attacks

Best practices include verifying the sender's email address, hovering over links to check their true destination, being cautious of unexpected attachments, and using two-factor authentication...
